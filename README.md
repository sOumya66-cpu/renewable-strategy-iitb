# Renewable Energy Grid Integration Strategy

Strategic consulting project focused on improving the reliability, scalability, and profitability of renewable energy systems in India. Achieved 2nd runner-up in the Pan-IIT finals hosted at IIT Bombay.

## Problem

Renewable energy adoption is constrained by intermittency, high storage costs, grid congestion, and operational inefficiencies.

## Solution

Developed a multi-pronged strategy combining Virtual Power Plants (VPPs), three-tier energy storage, grid modernization, and distributed solar services to improve renewable energy utilization and grid stability.

## Key Work

* Conducted industry and market analysis
* Performed PESTEL, SWOT, and Porter's Five Forces assessments
* Evaluated solutions using RICE and feasibility frameworks
* Built implementation roadmaps and financial projections
* Defined KPIs, risk mitigation plans, and growth opportunities
